ROOKIE RULE

Rosters may also include rookies that fall under the rookie rule. There is 1 spot available for players that are termed rookies.

  • Once a player that was deemed a rookie is inserted into a lineup he immediately loses his rookie status and a player must be dropped in order to make room for him
  • Practice spots are for players who are rookies from the year before and were kept as a keeper before the draft. Once a rookie has been played, they may not be put back onto your practice roster and you need to drop a player.

Failure to comply punishments:

1st Offense -25%
2nd Offense -50%
3rd Offense -100%
4th Offense FLOGGING

LEAGUE FEES

League fees $80. All initial yearly start-up costs will be due before or on draft day (a date to be announced soon). There will be NO EXCEPTIONS to this rule. If you have not taken care of your league fees by the start of the draft, you will not be permitted to draft until all other teams have finalized their respective rosters.

TRADING DEADLINE

Trading Deadline will be the Thursday of US Thanksgiving at midnight. Trading Frenzy will take place at the Prescott (or Scott's Hot Tub) starting at 2:00 PM on American Thanksgiving. Bring money and appetite. Trades must be completed by Midnight on Thanksgiving Thursday. All players traded before midnight who have not played or are currently playing will be traded immediately by the commissioner. Those players who have already finished their games will be traded the following week.

KEEPER RULE

Each team will be allowed to keep 3 players and 1 rookie from the year before, regardless if you have put them into your lineup or not. These keepers will need to be set 1 week before the draft date which is normally the 3rd week of preseason.

WAIVER PICKUPS

When picking up players off waivers, especially during the waiver period, you must drop players when picking up, unless you have open spots, or you are picking up a player who is currently on IR and you have space to put him there.
